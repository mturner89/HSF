Summary Event Schedule:

  • Full orientation on Wednesday May 20th, 2015 at 6:00PM located at Leander Boat Club.
  • One 2 hour lesson each week for 8 weeks beginning the week of May 25th, 2015 5:45-8:30PM.
  • Monday through Friday row times available and based on a first come first serve basis.
  • Regatta Sunday, July 19th, 2015.
  • Lunch Sponsored by Boston Pizza Main St West.
  • Grand Prize contest winner will be announced at the Heart and Stroke Regatta at an estimated time of 11:30AM

Fees:

Registration is $205.00 per rower. Fee is payable by cash or cheque, made out to Leander Boat Club, and given to the Hamilton Heart and Stroke Foundation Office: 7-1439 Upper Ottawa St, Hamilton.
